Cabbage Collector

A random number will be shown at the start of the game. Clicking a cabbage will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if you go over the random number.

The value of each cabbage is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the cabbage's values will change. Good luck!
